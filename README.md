## 🚀 From Hardware to Tensors

My journey began with a pile of old computers in Madagascar, taking things apart to understand how they worked. Today, I apply that same builder’s mindset to pixels and neural networks. 

I believe the most sophisticated model is useless if it’s invisible to the people who need it. That’s why I engineer robust **3D deep learning pipelines** and serve them through lightweight **WebXR interfaces**, allowing clinicians to interact with 3D medical data directly in the browser.
