# 👋 Hello, I'm Romualdo SEBANY

### AI Researcher | 3D Medical Imaging & WebXR

I operate at the convergence of deep learning and immersive spatial environments. My work bridges the gap between complex volumetric data and intuitive, browser-based clinical tools.

---

## 🚀 The Journey: From Hardware to Tensors

My path to AI didn't start in a high-tech lab; it started with a pile of old computers in Madagascar and a curiosity about how to make them useful again. That habit—taking things apart to understand how they work—remains the core engine of my engineering philosophy.

Today, as an AI Research Intern at the **Centre de Développement des Technologies Avancées (CDTA)**, I trade in pixels and tensors rather than physical hardware. I focus on developing end-to-end deep learning pipelines that transform raw, complex multi-dimensional medical imaging into structured, high-precision quantitative analysis.

---

## 🔬 Core Architectural Philosophy

> **"The most sophisticated model is useless if it remains invisible to the people who need it most."**

I believe that clinical AI must be accessible. To achieve this, I combine robust deep learning backends with WebXR frontends, allowing clinicians and researchers to interact with complex 3D structures directly in the browser without specialized local software.
